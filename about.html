Trusted Pre Primary School in Ulhasnagar

Kids Kingdom is a trusted pre primary school in Ulhasnagar providing quality early childhood education. We provide Playgroup, Nursery, Junior KG and Senior KG programs in a safe and caring environment.

Our classrooms are colorful and child-friendly. We use activity-based learning methods that help children develop creativity and confidence.

Kids Kingdom organizes an Annual Day every year which helps students build confidence and stage performance skills.

From the Principal Head's Desk

Welcome to Kids Kingdom Preschool. We aim to provide the best early education experience for every child. Our focus is on building confidence, communication skills and creativity.
